## 🧭 Planning Your Website

### Steps to Follow

- Create goals for your site
- Organize the site structure
- Create your design
- Design the navigation scheme
- Plan and gather your assets

⚠️ Plan everything **before starting development**

---

## 🎯 1. Create Goals for Your Site

- Decide what your website should achieve
- Discuss with your team
- Write down goals clearly
- Goals help focus your design and features
- Different goals → different design & navigation
- Complex goals → more complex navigation

---

## 🗂️ 2. Organize the Site Structure

- Create a proper folder structure
- Work on a **local site first** (not live site)
- Test before publishing
- Group related pages into folders
- Decide locations for images, media, etc.

---

## 🎨 3. Create Your Design

- Plan layout before development
- Use mockups (paper or tools)
- Maintain **consistent design across pages**
- Avoid confusing layouts
- Keep navigation placement consistent

---

## 🧭 4. Design Navigation Scheme

- Ensure easy movement between pages
- Keep navigation consistent
- Show users their current location
- Provide return to home/top page
- Add search/index for better usability

---

## 📦 5. Plan and Gather Assets

Assets include:

- Text
- Images
- Media files

✔ Collect all assets before development  
✔ Organize them properly in folders

---

- Home

## 🏠 Home Page

- Short introduction of the project
- Attractive design
- Use relevant graphics

### Design Tips:

- Avoid too many images
- Keep layout consistent
- Use optimized images (small size)
- Include navigation aids
